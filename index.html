Welcome to our portal

1: THIS PORTAL IS TO CONNECT VOLUNTEERS WITH ELDERLY PEOPLE SEEKING FOR HELP

This portal connects volunteers with patients and elderly individuals who need care — from simple companionship to daily assistance — all offered without monetary exchange. It promotes a community of compassion, where giving your time once makes you eligible to receive help in the future. By encouraging service before support, we aim to build a cycle of empathy, dignity, and mutual care.

2: WHY JOIN? BECAUSE GIVING COMES BACK IN FULL CIRCLE.

For Volunteers: Make a real impact by offering time, care, and kindness to those who need it most. Earn future priority access to similar caregiving support, simply by volunteering once. Experience the emotional fulfillment of helping another human being — without financial obligations. Join a network of compassionate individuals who believe in community, humanity, and service. Develop life-enriching skills in caregiving, empathy, communication, and patience.

For Consumers of Service: Receive genuine assistance and emotional support, especially in vulnerable times. Get matched with volunteers suited to your needs — whether for light companionship or daily activity assistance. Be part of a system that values kindness over currency, where help is given from the heart. If you’ve served as a volunteer yourself, you’ll be prioritized for care in your time of need.

3: DESCRIPTION OF EXPECTED USERS

Volunteers: Are you someone with time, empathy, and a desire to help? This portal is for individuals who want to offer non-professional support such as: Friendly companionship to those who feel isolated Assisting with daily routines, such as walking, feeding, reading, or light chores Accompanying someone to a hospital or appointment No medical training is required — only compassion, reliability, and respect for the dignity of others.

Consumers of Service (Patients / Elderly / Families): If you or your loved one is in need of: Simple companionship to combat loneliness Support in everyday tasks due to age or illness You can request a volunteer through this portal. Priority will be given to those who have previously volunteered, creating a system built on reciprocity and shared responsibility. Partner Organizations / NGOs We welcome partnerships with NGOs, senior care homes, community outreach teams, and hospitals. Use this platform to: Mobilize and manage volunteers Coordinate caregiving efforts efficiently Join a growing movement focused on human dignity, community care, and non-monetary service
